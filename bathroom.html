Service Includes:

  1. Elimination of Stubborn Stains:
    • Eradication of Persistent Dirt and Grime:
      • Special techniques and solutions to dissolve and remove ingrained dirt.
      • Focus on high-traffic areas that accumulate the most grime.
    • Removal of Black Stains in Corners:
      • Targeted cleaning to address discoloration in hard-to-reach corners.
      • Use of specialized tools to ensure thorough cleaning.
  2. Comprehensive Cleaning of Hard-to-Reach Areas:
    • Detailed Cleaning Behind the WC (Water Closet):
      • Removal of dust, dirt, and residues from behind the toilet.
      • Ensuring sanitation in areas often overlooked during routine cleaning.
    • Thorough Sanitation of Elevated and Concealed Spots:
      • Cleaning the tops of geysers, cabinets, and other high surfaces.
      • Use of ladders and extension tools to reach and clean these areas effectively.
  3. Final Touches:
    • Complete Drying with Multi-Fiber Cloth:
      • Ensuring every surface is left dry and streak-free.
      • Use of absorbent multi-fiber cloths to prevent water spots and streaks.
    • Application of Refreshing Fragrance Spray:
      • Spraying a pleasant scent to leave the bathroom smelling fresh and clean.
      • Selection of fragrances that are mild and not overpowering.
  4. Additional Services:
    • Mold and Mildew Treatment:
      • Application of anti-mold solutions to prevent and treat mold and mildew.
      • Ensuring long-lasting protection against fungal growth.
    • Polishing of Fixtures and Fittings:
      • Polishing taps, showerheads, and other metal fixtures to a shine.
      • Use of non-abrasive polishes to maintain the integrity of the fittings.
    • Grout Cleaning and Whitening:
      • Deep cleaning of grout lines to remove dirt and stains.
      • Application of grout whitening products for a renewed look.
  5. Sanitization and Disinfection:
    • Use of Hospital-Grade Disinfectants:
      • Ensuring all surfaces are free from harmful bacteria and viruses.
      • Focus on high-touch areas like door handles and switches.
    • Air Freshening and Purification:
      • Use of air purifiers and fresheners to enhance the overall atmosphere.
      • Ensuring the bathroom air is clean and pleasant.
